## Shooting Notes
- **The red-to-green ratio must be extreme** — at minimum 8:1 red to green. The compression of failure makes the single green cell land harder. If your tracker doesn't have enough rejections, scroll so the screen is full of red before the click
- **The cursor hover in Shot 2 must be a deliberate, visible pause** — this is the suspense node from the source video's dropdown moment. Do not edit it out thinking it's dead air. It is the hook
- **No face, no voiceover needed** — text overlay carries the entire narrative. Background music handles the emotional energy (aim for something that builds quietly — original video used a sped-up track)
- **Do not show anything after the green cell appears** — no celebration, no scrolling up, no reveal of the full tracker stats. The single green surrounded by red IS the ending
- **Notion or any visual tracker works** — Airtable, Google Sheets, even a custom spreadsheet. What matters is the color contrast: red = rejected, green = accepted
