## Shooting Notes

- **Lock the tripod.** Not a single pixel of drift. The emotional weight comes entirely from stillness — any camera shake breaks the illusion.
- **One take, no cuts.** This is a continuous 5–6 second clip. Do not edit between micro-expressions.
- **Text timing:** The full overlay text appears at 0:00 and stays pinned on screen for the entire video. Do not animate it in word by word.
- **Lighting:** Soft natural window light or a ring light aimed off-axis. The frame needs to feel like a bedroom, not a content studio.
- **Background props:** One or two personality items visible (a mug, a poster, a plant). Avoid blank walls. The signal is "real 24-year-old's room," not "brand shoot."
- **No BGM or voice:** Match the "original sound" approach from the source — silence amplifies the emotional weight and performs well in no-sound scroll environments.
