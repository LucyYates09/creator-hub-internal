## Hook
Just a friendly reminder that spending 20 minutes manually filling out the same job application form — over and over — is a complete waste of time.

**Why this hook works:** "Friendly reminder" sarcasm + named time-cost (20 minutes) activates collective job-seeker fatigue and FOMO, forcing viewers to stay for the "right answer"

## Shooting Notes
- Shots 1 & 3: Film against a window with natural-light blinds — same warm, lived-in look as source; kills the "studio ad" feel instantly
- Shot 2: Use a real Workday or Greenhouse screen recording, not a mock-up — authentic UI is the credibility; slow the scroll to make the pain feel long
- Shot 4: Natural hand shake is good; the 10–15% pull-back during "all you do is" mirrors the source's hand-held reveal beat exactly
- Shots 5–6: Screen-record live JobWizard autofill, speed up to 2–3x, then hard-cut with motion blur between shots 5 and 6 — copies the source's jump-cut energy burst
- Shot 7: Freeze on the completed form with match score legible for a full beat — a recognizable company name or high match percentage is the silent payoff; this is your "$132k RTX card" moment
