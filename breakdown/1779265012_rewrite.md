## Shooting Notes
- **Sankey / funnel animation is non-negotiable** — the data visualization drives completion rate. Each number must appear as a separate reveal, not all at once. Use Motion, Jitter, or Canva animation
- **"Shout out to JobWizard" is one sentence at ~12s** — say it and immediately continue to the next number. Don't stop to explain the product. Same move the source video makes
- **0.5s pause after every key number** — forces the viewer's brain to process and "do the math," which increases engagement and completion rate
- **The "200 applications in a day" line (Shot 8) is the payoff** — this is where the opening "500 in two weeks" mystery gets answered. Let that line land clearly
- **Screen recording must use the real JobWizard UI** on an actual job listing. No mockups. North American audiences spot staged screens immediately
- **Tone is "chill person explaining something that worked"** throughout — if you sound sad about the ghosting OR excited about the product, reshoot that shot
