## Shooting Notes
- Hand-hold the screen shot — no tripod, no screen recording. The wrist shake is the "this is real" credibility signal; a locked-off frame reads as a produced ad.
- Angle the laptop shot from above-left at roughly 20–30°, not perpendicular to the screen — matches the source's documentary framing.
- The dropdown in Shot 3 must be legible on-screen; zoom or crop close enough that "Offer Received" is readable before the click.
- Use natural window or overhead light for Shot 1 — avoid ring light. Ring light kills the emotional frame.
- JobWizard extension icon should be visible but ambient in Shots 2–3. It's proof-of-use, not a product demo moment. Do not zoom into it.
